Dune

Beyond fear, destiny awaits.

Plot

A mythic and emotionally charged hero's journey, "Dune" tells the story of Paul Atreides, a brilliant and gifted young man born into a great destiny beyond his understanding, who must travel to the most dangerous planet in the universe to ensure the future of his family and his people. As malevolent forces explode into conflict over the planet's exclusive supply of the most precious resource in existence-a commodity capable of unlocking humanity's greatest potential-only those who can conquer their fear will survive.

Key information

Director: Denis Villeneuve
Writer: Jon Spaihts, Denis Villeneuve, Eric Roth (screenplay); Frank Herbert (novel)
Release date: October 22, 2021 (United States)
Running time: 155 minutes
Budget: $165 million
Box office: $400 million
Cast: Timothée Chalamet, Rebecca Ferguson, Oscar Isaac, Josh Brolin, Stellan Skarsgård, Dave Bautista, Zendaya, Jason Momoa, Javier Bardem
Awards: "Dune" won six Academy Awards in 2022, for Best Cinematography, Best Film Editing, Best Original Score, Best Production Design, Best Sound, and Best Visual Effects.

Cast

Timothée Chalamet

Timothée Chalamet is a rising star in Hollywood, known for his intense and captivating performances. He has received critical acclaim for his roles in various films, including "Call Me by Your Name" and "Little Women." In "Dune," Chalamet delivers a powerful performance as Paul Atreides, capturing the character's complexity, determination, and evolving strength.

Rebecca Ferguson

Rebecca Ferguson is a talented actress with a diverse portfolio, known for her roles in the "Mission: Impossible" series and "The Greatest Showman." In "Dune," Ferguson plays Lady Jessica, Paul’s mother and a member of the Bene Gesserit sisterhood. Her portrayal is both intense and compassionate, reflecting the character’s inner strength and devotion to her son.

Oscar Isaac

Oscar Isaac is a versatile actor recognized for his roles in "Ex Machina," "Inside Llewyn Davis," and the "Star Wars" sequel trilogy. In "Dune," Isaac portrays Duke Leto Atreides, the noble leader of House Atreides. His performance is authoritative and heartfelt, embodying the character's leadership qualities and deep care for his family and people.

Characters

Paul Atreides

Paul Atreides, played by Timothée Chalamet, is the protagonist of the film and the heir to House Atreides. He is a young man with a destiny that he does not fully understand but must navigate to survive and fulfill. Throughout the film, Paul evolves from a reluctant heir to a confident leader, embracing his potential as the prophesied Kwisatz Haderach.

Lady Jessica

Lady Jessica, portrayed by Rebecca Ferguson, is Paul’s mother and the concubine of Duke Leto. A member of the mystical Bene Gesserit sisterhood, she has trained Paul in their ways, hoping he will be the prophesied superbeing. Her character is depicted as both fiercely protective and deeply committed to her son's destiny.

Duke Leto Atreides

Duke Leto Atreides, played by Oscar Isaac, is the noble and honorable head of House Atreides. He is entrusted with the stewardship of Arrakis and its valuable spice production. Leto's character is marked by his strong sense of duty, leadership, and his genuine love for his family and subjects, making him a beloved and respected leader.
